# Welcome to Radium

Radium is your launchpad for rapid project development. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, our platform provides the tools and resources you need to bring your ideas to life quickly and efficiently.

## Getting Started

1. **Install create-radium CLI**:

   ```
   bunx create-radium@latest
   ```

2. **Initialize Your Project**: follow the prompts to set up your project.

3. **Explore Templates**:
   Browse our collection of templates to find the perfect starting point for your project.

4. **Leverage UI Libraries**:
   Integrate our UI components to build sleek and responsive interfaces with minimal effort.

## Why Radium?

- **Speed**: Rapidly prototype and deploy your projects.
- **Flexibility**: Choose from a variety of templates and customize to your needs.
- **Community**: Join a growing ecosystem of developers and contributors.

Start building with Radium today and experience the future of rapid development!
