# About Radium

Radium is an open-source platform designed to empower developers with tools and services for rapid project deployment. Our mission is to streamline the development process, allowing you to focus on what matters most: bringing your ideas to life.

## Key Features

- **create-radium CLI**: Initialize your projects with ease using our command-line interface tool.
- **Templates**: Jump-start your development with our curated collection of project templates.
- **UI Libraries**: Access a rich set of UI components to build beautiful and functional interfaces.

At Radium, we believe in the power of open-source collaboration and the creativity of the developer community. Our platform is built to support your journey from concept to production, providing the resources you need to ship your projects faster and more efficiently.
